## IO Distribution Guidelines
The IO distribution has been designed to reward and empower partners who actively contribute to the IOXtream ecosystem, fostering engagement and innovation. The IO NFTs for this launch program are allocated as follows:

- **147 IO: Reserved for our Treasury (Only for our team members and partners)**
- **630 IO: Allocated for public minting**

**TOTAL IO Agents: 777 NFTs**

### Eligibility Rules for IO Partners Distribution

To ensure fair distribution, the eligibility criteria are divided into two categories: **Streamers/Xtreamers** and **Blockchain Content Creators**. Below are the specific requirements for each group.

### 1. For Streamers/Xtreamers

To qualify for an IO Agent NFT, streamers must meet the following conditions:

**Must-Haves:**

1. **Regular Live Streaming:**
    - Conduct at least 4 live streams per month consistently for the last 2 months before applying.

2. **Engagement with IOXtream on Social Media:**
    - Like, share, and comment on posts from [@IOXtream](https://x.com/IOXtream).
    - Additionally, be regularly active on the official IOXtream Discord server and help promote the project.

3. **Active IOXtream Donation Tools:**
    - Prominently display your IOXtream donation page:
        - Have OverlayX configured and active.
        - Include it in your channel panels (e.g., Twitch or YouTube descriptions).
        - Display a QR Code overlay visibly during live streams.

4. **Cardano Wallet Address:**
    - Provide a valid Cardano wallet address to receive the NFT.
    - If you donâ€™t have one yet, contact our team on Discord for assistance.

**Nice-to-Haves (Boost Your Chances):**

1. **Social Media Tagging:**

    Include [@ioxtream](https://x.com/IOXtream) in your profile and bio on your social media accounts.

2. **Community Engagement:**

    Regularly tag [@ioxtream](https://x.com/IOXtream) in announcements or live stream highlights to interact with the community.

3. **Content Creation:**

    Post social media content (especially on X) about how IOXtream improves engagement in your live streams.

4. **Active Experiences:**

    Have smart devices connected to IOXtream experiences.

### 2. For Blockchain Content Creators and Web3 Project Owners
Blockchain content creators and Web3 project owners play a key role in expanding the IOXtream ecosystem. To be eligible for an IO Agent NFT, they must meet the following criteria:

**Must-Haves:**

1. **Project Demonstration:**
    - Present an active project you are part of or have helped promote.
    - Show innovation and utility within the Cardano ecosystem and demonstrate a meaningful connection with IOXtream.

2. **Integration with IOXtream:**
    - Integrate or propose an integration of IOXtream tools (e.g., donations, gamified rewards) into your blockchain solutions.

3. **Community Engagement:**
    - Host at least one community session (e.g., AMA, live tutorial, or workshop) showcasing IOXtream to your audience on X and/or Discord.

4. **Valid Cardano Wallet:**
    - Provide a valid Cardano wallet address to receive the NFT.

**Nice-to-Haves (Boost Your Chances):**

1. **Educational Content:**
    
    Publish at least one video or article explaining how IO Agents will revolutionize interactive live experiences.

### Application Process
If you meet the requirements to receive an IO Agent NFT from our Treasury, join our Discord to chat with our team or send an email to partnership@ioxtream.com.

By following these guidelines, both Xtreamers and Blockchain Content Creators can contribute to a thriving ecosystem, being rewarded for their dedication.
