## Mission

Our mission is to bring magic to the masses by creating multisensory, interactive, and scalable experiences that transform physical spaces into unforgettable journeys, fostering deeper connections between people and brands.

## Vision

To be the global leader in interactive technology, redefining how people experience spaces and brands by making the extraordinary an everyday possibility.

## Values

- ### Innovation First:
  We challenge norms and embrace bold ideas to continuously redefine the boundaries of interactive experiences in the long term.

- ### Empowering Decentralized Collaboration:
  We believe that decentralization and open source unlock the full potential of collective human creativity.

- ### Human Connection:
  Our experiences are designed to bring people closer to each other and the brands they love.

- ### Customer-Centricity:
  The needs and dreams of our community, clients and their audiences are at the heart of everything we do.

- ### Integrity:
  We build trust through transparency and delivering on our promises.
